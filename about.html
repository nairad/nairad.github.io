What is Pictobaby?

Pictobaby is a reading game that employs the technology of the Makey Makey to teach kids how to read and recognize everyday words. It's easy to set up and even easier to play!

How it works

The Makey Makey connects to any laptop and acts like your keyboard. The only difference is that it can recieve input from... two pieces of Play-Doh!
Your child sees an image and two words, colored differently, on the computer screen. In front of them is the Makey Makey, attached to which are two pieces of Play-Doh, in corresponding colors to the words on screen. Only one word matches the image, and your child selects which one they think it is by pressing the piece of Play-Doh that corresponds to their answer. It's as easy as that!
